Hi, and welcome

A quick little intro.. I was born and raised in the seaside city of Plymouth, way back when music had rhythm but fashion was a bit of a disaster. Married with 4 children, my wife is my rock, my children my legacy.

Over 20 years experience in the IT sector in various roles, support, technical, managerial and operational. Web development started as a bit of an accident, just as a general interest a few years ago as something to keep me occupied. Then you enter the rabbit hole....
